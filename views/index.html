Why use Event Points?

If you're attending an event

  • Scan codes to get points
  • Transfer points to and from your friends (if you want)

If you're organizing an event

  • Increase engagement by incentivizing interactions

Increase engagement with localized digital currency (points), by letting your guests scan QR codes. Super simple: scan codes, get points. You decide how your guests should redeem their points, and you're in control
